About

East Kent Pokémon Go is a community covering Margate, Westgate, Birchington, Ramsgate, Broadstairs and Cliftonville. Chat with other players in the area, make friends, raid, compete in our leaderboards. Everyone is welcome, Come and join the fun!

If you join this community, you can expect:

  • Multiple raids every day.
  • Leaderboards, compete with other players to see who has the most XP.
  • EX raids.
  • Trading.
  • Events.
  • Chat with other players.
  • And much much more.

How to join

We use Discord, the text chat app for gamers. You can get it from the App store or Google Play.



Once you've got the app and signed up, come back here and press the Join Server button at the top of the page. Be sure to head to the #verification channel and post a screenshot of your profile page. You can access your profile page by pressing the button on the bottom left in Pokémon Go.

Rules

Pokémon Go East Kent is a community of like-minded players, and as such we have some rules we'd like you to follow while playing.

  • No insulting other members. Please be civil!
  • Falsifying your location will NOT be tolerated in this group. We have a three strike policy and found spoofers will be banned.
  • No spam, no porn, no abusing mentions. References to porn websites or illegal drugs will result in a warning.
  • Accounts must be verified within a month, otherwise they will be removed. If you're under 18, you will have to declare so upon joining. Verification is handled by TrainerDex, more information can be found at trainerdex.co.uk. To verify, head to #verification and read the message posted by @Dyno
  • No bullying or ganging up on other members of the group.
